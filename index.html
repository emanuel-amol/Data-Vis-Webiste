The Story of Road Safety Enforcement in Australia

Did you know that adults aged 40-64 receive more road safety fines than any other age group in Australia, challenging the common perception that younger drivers are the primary offenders?

Our data visualization explores 15 years of road safety enforcement data to uncover patterns that can help improve safety strategies and save lives.

Key Insights from Our Analysis

604,750 Fines issued to the 40-64 age group, the highest of any demographic
2021 The year with the highest number of recorded fines, showing a 37% increase from 2020
VIC & NSW The jurisdictions with the highest enforcement rates, accounting for over 60% of all fines

Project Overview

This interactive data visualization provides insights into road safety enforcement data collected by the Bureau of Infrastructure and Transport Research Economics (BITRE) from 2008 to 2023.

Our visualization helps policy makers, law enforcement agencies, researchers, and the general public understand patterns in road safety violations to make data-driven decisions for better road safety outcomes.
